How it works.  The vector field samples ẋ = f(x) on a grid; each arrow points along the local flow with length proportional to speed |ẋ|. Trajectories are integrated with fixed-step 4th-order Runge–Kutta from the initial condition you click. Every trace stays until you Clear. All client-side — no server, no install.
